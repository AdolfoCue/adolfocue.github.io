ABOUT ME

I look to make the world better by participating in it.

Music was my first love. In Miami Lakes Tech, the highschool division, I studied music theory and sound engineering. Eventually, graduated with a degree in classical guitar and music education. Moved to Portland in August of 2007 and toured the country for 6 years with a band named NIAYH (Now Is All You Have). In between tours, Marc Greco, the founder of N3 Notary and a dear friend of mine, decided to hire me on and ended up coaching me on the core elements of sales and running a business. The entreprenuer bug bit me so I started a business in Real Estate. Currently, I own my own agency (Adolfostreet.com). Seeing the influence of companies like Redfin and Faira, I recognized the urgency of becoming relevant to the tech industry by learning and creating in the Real Estate Technology space. Here I am today...
